    What is EbriiCare™?

     First Responder's Best Friend
    During emergency medical crisis, we ensure that first responders will have an access to your medical records and history to avoid medical errors that may lead to infection, allergies and other unnecessary medical malpractice. We also ensure that medical responders will know the best way to treat you and reach out your loved ones in case of dire emergency.
     Accessible Patient Records
    Upon sign up, you can add your personal and medical information, upload medical prescriptions and other laboratory results for future references. You will be requested to add any prescription details that first responders might need to know if any case you are caught in trouble. This will also be accessed by the medical responders so they can check if you have any underlying medical concerns that should be brought up. Your doctor can also track your record for future appointments.
